The Goldilock System

The Goldilock system is a system/zone located 494 light years away from Earth. It's known to be a habital zone with planets that should be able to support liquid water.
Since the planets are situated very close to each other(shown on the right hand side's picture), gravitational forces are affected. Additionally seeing other planets in the sky while on one of those planets isn't uncommon.





Super Habitable Planets


Yup! It's pretty much how it sounds like. Super Habitable Planets are planets that are extremely habitable, that may be better suited than Earth in regards to supporting life, or evolutions of it.
A discovered planet known as a Super Havitable Planet is called: Kepler-442b. It's slightly larger than Earth, and seemingly has a habitable zone.
With The James Webb Telescope up in space and getting ready to start looking out into space, this powerful telescope can tell us if there may be life on other planets, or what other mysteries may lie in deep space.
